2 eggs, 500ml of milk, grated cheese,aromat & pepper,mushrooms,baby tomatoes,butter

Mix eggs with milk and aromat for taste

put butter in the pan and let it fry

Pour eggs in hot oil and let them cook for a short while

Mix cheese in half done eggs and allow to cook for 2 minutes

Serve eggs with Toast/Bread and enjoy with Juice,Coffee or Tea
